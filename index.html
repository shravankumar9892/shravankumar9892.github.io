Finance

I've completed 3 courses on Quantitative Research and 2 ongoing courses on Probability and Statistics for Data Science.

In "Management and financial accounting" I learnt about the accounting theory, finance theory. I learnt about the 4 basic financial statements for business. I learnt about several ratios for interpreting the performance of an organization, the "Cash Flow Analysis Framework". In the management part of the course I learnt about the Strategy of an organization, interpreting accounting and financial ratios to drive better internal decisions and management accounting. I learnt about setting SMART KPIs, Agency Theory. In the last 3 weeks they taught us about Budgeting, Forecasting and Modelling.

In the course "Introduction to Corporate Finance" by Wharton, I learnt about Time Value of Money (Discounting, Compunding, Annuity, Perpetuity, Taxes), Interest rates, Relationship between APR and EAR, Term structure. In week 3, they taught us about Discounted Cash Flow Analysis, Forecasting Free Cash flows. In the last week they taught us about Return on Investment.
